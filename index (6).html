Bench Tech Needs

CAN Bus Gauge Sweep
Full 8-gauge sweep over ISO 11898 CAN at 125k–500kbps. Speed, RPM, coolant, fuel, oil pressure, voltage, trans temp, and odometer. Auto sweep or manual hold per gauge.
All Plans
📡
GM Class 2 J1850 VPW
Full J1850 VPW protocol support for GM vehicles 1994–2003. 5 Class 2 gauges including the GM-specific cluster messages at 10.4kbps. No extra hardware needed.
All Plans
🔬
EEPROM Decoder
Paste a raw hex dump and instantly see decoded odometer, VIN, engine hours, config bytes, and checksum — with a color-coded byte map. Supports GM Delco, Ford Visteon, Chrysler, and Toyota Denso.
Pro + Shop
✏️
Odometer & Hours Encoder
Enter your target mileage, business miles, personal miles, engine hours, and idle hours. Get back write-ready hex bytes for every cluster family simultaneously. Copy to clipboard in one click.
Pro + Shop
🛣️
Drive Cycle Simulator
6-phase realistic drive cycle: cold start → idle → city → highway → decel → park. Simulates real cluster behavior over time to catch intermittent gauge and lighting faults before the cluster ships.
All Plans
📄
PDF Test Reports
Generate a dated pass/fail test report with part number, test results, gauge readings, and a QR code. Perfect for eBay listings, shop records, and proving the cluster was bench-verified before sale.
